Student Tutor for CS4243-AI Decision-making and Planning.

I served as a student tutor for an undergraduate Artificial Intelligence (AI) course titled AI Decision-making and Planning . My responsibilities included conducting tutorials 3 times a week, holding consultation sessions, and grading examination papers. At the end of the semester, I received a score of 4.4 out 5.0 based on student feedback which secured me a place in the prestigious Honor List of Student Tutors.

E111 System 800xA Virtualization of Server Nodes

I developed a new course on using VMware virtualization software for ABB's System 800xA. The course spanned over one week and was intended for ABB customers and engineers. I prepared the training material and exercises, as well as online recordings. I travelled to ABB branches globally to conduct the training. Course details can be found at the ABB Library website.

Expert trainer for System 800xA troubleshooting courses

I travelled to ABB branches round the globe to conduct advanced level troubleshooting courses to customers and ABB engineers. In particular, I conduced E143: System 800xA-Troubleshooting Core System, and E144: System 800xA-Performing a System Health Check in countries including Germany, Sweden, United States of America, Australia, India, and Singapore.
